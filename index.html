Thunder Spectra

The Puma Thunder Spectra is a retro-inspired lifestyle silhouette originally introduced in April 2018. Featuring bold color blocking, a mixture of materials from mesh to leather, and a chunky silhouette perfectly in line with 2018’s immensely popular “dad shoe” trend, the Thunder Spectra pushes Puma’s classic lifestyle footwear in a bold new direction.
